Hyperlinks

Hyperlinks are basically links that user that click on to get to another HTML page.
They are called anchor elements in HTML and there are two types of Hyperlinks.

  • External links are hyperlink that points to any domain

  • Internal links are any links from one page on your website to another page on your website


What are Semantic Elements?

A Semantic Element clearly describes its meaning to both the browser and the developer.

  • Non-Semantic elements: are div and span - Tells nothing about its content.
  • Semantic Elements: are form, table, and article - Clearly defines its content.
    The benefits of using Semantic elements over no Semantic elements are
    • Easier to read your code
    • Better fdor search result
    • Better for screen readers
